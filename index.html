About Revisio

Revisio is a web app for text revisions, but not like a typical text editor. Its purpose is to help you focus on creation rather than manipulation. It gives you a bird's eye view of your text so you can easily achieve good writing flow. It aids you in your change choices. And allows you to copy the revised text back to your main text editing program.

The app is useful for sorting out the flow of the text by easily changing sentence positions. It helps you decide if your changes are better and worth keeping by keeping a history of them. The editing is done swiftly with keyboard shortcuts. The boring sorting manipulations of the text are automated. And the data is saved automatically in the cache of your browser.

Selection

"UpArrow" — Go to Previous Sentence

"DownArrow" — Go to Next Sentence

"PageUp" — Go to Previous Paragraph

"PageDown" — Go to Next Paragraph

"Home" — Go to Beginning of Paragraph

"End" — Go to End of Paragraph

"Ctrl + Home" — Go to Beginning of Editor

"Ctrl + End" — Go to End of Editor

Movement

"Shift + UpArrow" — Move Sentence Up

"Shift + DownArrow" — Move Sentence Down

"Shift + PageUp" — Move Sentence to Previous Paragraph

"Shift + PageDown" — Move Sentence to Next Paragraph

"Shift + Home" — Move Sentence to Top of Paragraph

"Shift + End" — Move Sentence to Bottom of Paragraph

"Ctrl + Shift + Home" — Move Sentence to Top of Revision

"Ctrl + Shift + End" — Move Sentence to Bottom of Revision

Editing

"RightArrow" — Show Sentence Variants

"LeftArrow" — Hide Sentence Variants

"Enter" — Add New Sentence / Variant

"Shift + Enter" — Add New Sentence Variant

"Delete" — Delete Sentence / Variant Permanently

"Space" — Choose Best Sentence Variant

Paragraphs

"Ctrl + <" — Move Paragraph Up

"Ctrl + >" — Move Paragraph Down

"Ctrl + Enter" — Split Paragraph in Two Paragraphs

"Ctrl + Backspace" — Combine Paragraph with Next Paragraph
