About Revisio

This app is not like a typical text editor. It is for revisions of raw written material for articles, essays, and even chapters or sections of text. Its purpose is to automate the boring copying and pasting of sentence-contents that you do in a typical text editor to sort them in the text. The app also allows you to rewrite each sentence-content and keeps a history of different versions of the sentence-contents, so you can choose the best ones.

How it works

  • The app splits the text into sentence-contents that you can easily sort to achieve a nice flow for reading.
  • You can write new versions of every sentence-content and compare it with the original sentence-content to choose the better one.
  • The app joins the sentence-contents in paragraphs for your convenience to read the revised text.
  • The app automatically saves your revision in the Local Storage of your browser.

Instructions

  1. Copy the raw text into the right panel and click the Tokenize button. The text is then split into sentence-contents.
  2. Use the mouse or Up and Down Arrow keys to select a sentence-content in the editor panel.
  3. Use the mouse to drag and drop or Alt+Up or Alt+Down keys to sort the sentence-contents.
  4. To insert a new sentence-content below the current one, press Alt+Enter.
  5. To write a new version of a sentence-content, press Enter to go into sentence-content editing mode. If there is only one version of the sentence-content, you will be prompted to write a new version. If there are already two versions of the sentence-content, you can select the better one with the Enter key. To edit a sentence-content version without writing a new version, double click with the mouse on it. Press the Esc key to exit sentence-content editing mode and show only the better version of the sentence-content.
  6. To add a new paragraph divider, press Alt+P.
  7. If you are happy with the revision, press the Copy button to copy the revised text from the preview panel.
  8. To clear the revision from the editor and the Local Storage of your browser, press the Clean button. Be careful because this step cannot be undone.
